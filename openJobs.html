JSE Acquisition Analyst

Job Location:

Hybrid with 3 days a week at the government facility at Pax River and 2 days a
week split between the Nine 30 office in Lexington Park and Work From Home (WFH)

Job Description:

Provide acquisition support to the Joint Simulation Environment (JSE) Division at Patuxent River
supporting service contract and hardware contract procurement actions.

Provide service acquisition support and develop acquisition strategies and plans by considering
current and future project needs. Develop Procurement Initiation Documents (PIDs) and track
Procurement Requests (PRs) through Navy ERP. Support CORs/ACORs and Acquisition Cell with
post award contract management across a portfolio of contracts. Support the Project Leads on
managing, monitoring, and reporting on specific projects Task Orders (TOs). Determine long
term acquisition and resource requirements.

Provide hardware acquisition support and monitor procurements to ensure project schedules
are met. Assist in translating technical requirements into acquisition documentation, conduct
market research, and develop Bill of Materials (BOM) and Material Equipment Lists (MELs).
Draft procurement documentation, track material from procurement planning to delivery, and
provide project material tracking reports to Acquisition Cell and Project Lead stakeholders.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in DoD acquisition management or
    procurement management
  • Proficiency in MS Office Tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in Navy ERP and Procurement Management Tool (PMT)

Data Scientist

Job Location:

Hybrid with primarily Work From Home (WFH), but with occasional meetings at
the Nine 30 office in Lexington Park, MD.

Job Description:

Provide data analytics support to the Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division (NAWCAD)
Comptroller and Business & Financial Management Community (BFMC) to provide real-time
data to improve organizational decision making.

We are looking for a Qlik Developer, Data Analyst, or Data Scientist with at least 3 years of
experience in business intelligence and at least one year of experience with Qlik to join our
Business Intelligence team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating,
implementing, and maintaining Qlik dashboards, reports, and data models. Work closely with
Comptroller, BFMs, project managers, financial analysts, and acquisition specialists to
understand their needs and requirements and apply these to the build/test phase of reports
and dashboards. Identify opportunities to improve data visualization tools and streamline
reporting to make processes more automated and less manual. Develop and manage business
intelligence solutions for the organization.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in business intelligence
  • Proficiency in Qlik
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Knowledge of DoD acquisition, financial, and DoD PPBE processes preferred

Project Management Analyst

Job Description:

Provide project management support in the initiating, planning, executing, monitoring &
controlling, and closing of projects.

Ensure the accuracy of project cost, schedule, and performance data to support project and
organizational decision making. Monitor commitment, obligation, and expenditure data from
Navy ERP and internal financial tracking tools and trackers, identify project risks, and develop
mitigation strategies. Develop sponsor Monthly Status Reports (MSRs) summarizing cost,
schedule, and technical health of projects. Maintain financial spreadsheets, databases, and
dashboards and monitor project authorized amounts, burn rates, and balances. Develop and
run recurring and ad hoc financial reports, review financial data on labor, travel, material,
and service contract transactions, and coordinate with internal project teams to execute project
funds.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in DoD project management or
    financial management
  • Proficiency in MS Office tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in Navy ERP is preferred
